OUR STORY

Cut from the city itself.

Chicago Code started in a Pilsen basement with a single screen press and a stubborn idea: clothes that could survive a Chicago winter and still look right on a summer rooftop. Every piece is designed in the city and built to last.

We run small batches, pay fair, and print local. No fast fashion, no filler — just gear we'd actually wear.
